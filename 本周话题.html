Equity in paediatric care for sexual and gender minority adolescents

Laura Baams
Published:May 14, 2021DOI:https://doi.org/10.1016/S2352-4642(21)00129-2
PlumX Metrics

On May 17, the International Day Against Homophobia, Biphobia, Intersexphobia, and Transphobia raises awareness of equality for lesbian, gay, bisexual, trans, queer, and intersex individuals. The day originally came about to commemorate the removal of homosexuality from the International Classification of Diseases. Despite ongoing progress towards equality and equity, sexual and gender minority individuals are still at risk because of mental health disparities, especially during the critical phase of adolescence—when they explore and form their distinct identities.
